Location: 9th St Abbey
1808 S 9th St, St. Louis, MO 63104
A German Abbey built in 1848 will serve as the venue for both the wedding ceremony and reception. It is located in the historic Soulard neighborhood where it stands as a landmark along-side classic restaurants like Boggart's Smokehouse, breweries like Anheuser-Busch, and one of the oldest Farmer's Markets in the United States. The balcony of the venue is not to be missed!
Mackenzie Britton
Role: Paladin
Arguing with Mark about Java code and the Sound of Music has turned into arguing with Neem about Purple vs Red in website design. Today they both won. Mackenzie and Neem are rarely spotted apart while travelling or exploring the wonderland of St Louis. Enamored with all things related to cats and board games, she is nothing short of a tremendous best friend.
Tim Katerberg
Role: Ranger
As comfortable hiking Glacier National Park as he is drinking a beer cheering on Michigan Football, Tim is a lesson in eclecticism. The fact that he has known the groom the longest of anyone is technically correct, which as Tim will tell you, is the best kind of correct. A mentor in all things technical, he has always inspired greatness from the people around him, and Mark is still trying to live up to the challenge.
Cait Pickens
Role: Rogue
Responding to the awkward Facebook message from a stranger turned out to be a fantastic choice for Cait. She and Neem became friends discussing teaching at technology conferences but they became great friends eating cake and ice cream under blanket forts. She is always there to make Neem comfortable with both of their quirkiness and she is truly a teacher that is it possible to be fabulous and brilliant at the same time.
Ian Tobasco
Role: Wizard
A jazz pianist, a mathematician, and a ultimate frisbee player walk into a bar and he orders a Silver Gimlet. Ian and Mark have been best friends since high school, usually playing Final Fantasy or Dance Dance Revolution when they should be studying. They backpacked and debated together, often at the same time. They both have a proclivity for scarves and obsessively arguing about irrelevant details. When you are wandering Ann Arbor, there is no better guide.
Bethany Katerkesa
Role: Bard
In-laws can often be cold, but Bethany was just cool. When she graciously allowed Neem to stay with her before Neem was anything more than a weird high-school friend of Mark's, she was cemented as the Kind Katerberg. No matter how deep a conversation gets with her, you can always be sure to come out feeling better about yourself. Introducing Neem to Frozen turned out to be a melodic nightmare for Mark, who had to hear songs about snowmen for months. Bethany does enough running for both her and Neem combined.
