Location: 9th St Abbey
1808 S 9th St, St. Louis, MO 63104
A German Abbey built in 1848 will serve as the venue for both the wedding ceremony and reception. It is located in the historic Soulard neighborhood where it stands as a landmark along-side classic restaurants like Boggart's Smokehouse, breweries like Anheuser-Busch, and one of the oldest Farmer's Markets in the United States. The balcony of the venue is not to be missed!
