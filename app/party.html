Craig Katerberg
Role: Fighter
Inscrutable. Both as a madeira maven and as a world traveller, Craig has been a trail-blazer for the bride and groom. He is tied for knowing Mark the longest in the wedding party and has far too many stories of embarrasing moments for Mark to feel comfortable when conversation drifts to his childhood. He introduced Mark to Magic: the Gathering for which Neem has never forgiven him.

Ian Tobasco
Role: Wizard
A jazz pianist, a mathematician, and a ultimate frisbee player walk into a bar and he orders a Silver Gimlet. Ian and Mark have been best friends since high school, usually playing Final Fantasy or Dance Dance Revolution when they should be studying. They backpacked and debated together, often at the same time. They both have a proclivity for scarves and obsessively arguing about irrelevant details. When you are wandering Ann Arbor, there is no better guide.
